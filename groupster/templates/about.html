How it works

1. Create a Crew

Each crew consists of up to four members. You can have multiple crews, whether it be your fraternity brothers, sorority sisters, or soccer friends you can diversify your crews. Choose your interested activities, like bars, clubbing, or restaurants. Once have set your discovery preferences and you have created a crew, you can begin liking other crews.

2. Schedule hangout

If there is a mutual interest between both crews, both crews get placed into a huge group chat where all members can participate. From there, the crew leader can schedule a hangout with the other crew leader and set the location and time to hangout.

3. Rate hangout

This is where it gets interesting! 24 hours after the scheduled hangout, all members can now rate the opposite group on how the hangout went. Dont worry! Your rating won't be displayed, however if you have above a certain cumulative rating, you now become "Groupster Certified", showing that your crew knows how to have fun!
